# AiExponent

**Building AI that deserves to be trusted.**

The EU AI Act is now law. Enforcement deadlines are active. AiExponent builds the open source tools and enterprise products that engineering teams need to ship compliant, responsible AI systems.

## Open Source Tools

| Tool | What it does | EU AI Act |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| [license-compliance-checker](https://github.com/aiexponenthq/license-compliance-checker) | Scans AI model licenses for GPAI compliance | Article 53 |
| [agentic-document-analyser](https://github.com/aiexponenthq/agentic-document-analyser) | Multi-agent document analysis for risk management | Article 9 |
| [rag-benchmarking](https://github.com/aiexponenthq/rag-benchmarking) | Systematic RAG evaluation for accuracy requirements | Article 15 |

All tools are **Apache 2.0 licensed**. More tools coming soon.
